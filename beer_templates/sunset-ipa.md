## The Story

Sunset IPA was born on a hot September afternoon when the light was golden and the hops were fresh. We wanted a beer that captured that feeling — warm, bright, and just a little bit wild.

## Brew Notes

Double dry-hopped with Citra and Mosaic for maximum aroma. Fermented cool at 18°C for a clean profile that lets the hops shine.
