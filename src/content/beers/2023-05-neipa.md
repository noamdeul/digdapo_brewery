## Brew Notes

Batch #6. OG: 1.048. FG: 1.014. Bottled on 2023-06-17. Addition: Mulberry concentrate


New England IPA recipe — a hazy, rich, fruity beer with balanced bitterness.

Beer style:
New England IPA (NEIPA) is a beer style that is a fresh, modern take on the classic IPA. The style is characterized by a hazy, cloudy texture that comes from high doses of wheat and unmalted grains, plus a strong aroma from the use of aromatic hops. New England IPA differs from the standard American IPA thanks to more pronounced fruity and tropical flavors and aromas, alongside relatively low and balanced bitterness. The thick texture and stronger aromas make it an excellent choice for IPA lovers.

Ingredients for 20 liters:

Pilsner malt extract

Grains:

Wheat: 300 g

Dextrin: 300 g

Vienna: 400 g

Hops and hop schedule:

Magnum: 15 g — added at 60 minutes of boil (for bitterness).

Centennial: 60 g — added as dry hop three days before bottling

Wolf: 60 g — added as dry hop three days before bottling

Yeast:

Verdant yeast (recommended for IPAs; this yeast contributes fruity and tropical aromas).

Recommended fermentation temperature:

17–23°C, for 14–21 days.
