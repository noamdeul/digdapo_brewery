## The Story

Our first stout was a winter project. We wanted something to warm the hands and the soul — dark, smooth, and deeply satisfying.

## Brew Notes

The oat addition gives this beer its signature silky mouthfeel. We kept the hop bitterness restrained to let the malt complexity speak.
