## Brew Notes



Beer brewing kit — West Coast IPA (20 liters)
Short description:
A classic version of the American IPA — a bitter, clear, reddish, super fruity beer with a pronounced and powerful hop profile.

Malt ingredients:
4.5 kg Pale Ale Malt

400 g Vienna Malt

150 g Crystal 240 Malt

200 g Dextrin Malt

Hop ingredients:
15 g Magnum hops — added at the start of the boil (60 minutes)

30 g Talus hops — added 5 minutes before the end of the boil

30 g Idaho 7 hops — added in the last minute of the boil

60 g Cascade hops — hop stand immediately after the end of the boil

Yeast:
One pack of VERDANT yeast

Brewing instructions:
Mash:
26 liters of water at 66°C for 60 minutes

Sparge:
Rinse with 3 liters of hot water (~75°C)

Boil:
Total boil time — 60 minutes
Hop additions and hop stand per the schedule above

Fermentation:
Ferment at 18°C for 14 days
Followed by 72 hours of cold crash at low temperature

Expected specs:

Value
ABV: 5.5%
Original gravity (OG): 1.050
Final gravity (FG): 1.013
Color (EBC): 6 (light golden-amber)
Bitterness (IBU): 31 (high, pronounced bitterness)
