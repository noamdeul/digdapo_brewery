## Brew Notes

Batch #4. OG: 1.058. FG: 1.016. Bottled on 2023-03-25.


APA beer recipe

An American take on Pale Ale

More fruity with lively, balanced bitterness

A beer full of fruity aromas

Simcoe — an American hop with tropical flavors and citrus aroma

El Dorado — an especially tropical American hop with notes of pineapple, peach, and melon

Recipe for 20 liters

 

Pale Ale extract: 3 kg

Grains:

CaraMalt 200

Pale Ale: 800 g

Hops:

Cascade: 30 g — first hop addition at 45 min

El Dorado: 30 g — last 5 min

Simcoe: 30 g — last 3 min

Optionally, add 60 g Simcoe separately as dry hop, three days before bottling.

Yeast: BRY97
